PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:
- **Core Goal:** Replicate DrugLift's feature set.
- **Key Features:**
  - Multi-role authentication (Admin, Pharmacy, Driver, Patient).
  - Pharmacy Portal: Dashboard, creating/managing deliveries, QR code scanning.
  - Driver Portal: View and manage assigned deliveries, scan packages, capture Proof of Delivery.
  - Admin Dashboard: Full oversight to manage users, pharmacies, deliveries, pricing, and view system-wide analytics.
  - Patient Experience: Real-time public tracking page.
- **Mandatory Integrations:** Circuit Spoke, Google Maps, Stripe, Twilio, SendGrid.
- **Admin Controls:**
    - Full control over user accounts (drivers, pharmacies).
    - Ability to change the status of any delivery at any time.
    - Control over pricing for delivery types:

        - Priority (first delivery of the day)
        - Refrigerated (add-on fee)
